Text Captchas

The majority of deployed captcha complications are based on distorted letters or numbers, and identifying images with these characteristics is a common approach. Our attack against the image captcha scheme can be readily applied to the second and third type without modification.

Image Captchas

Distorted letters or numbers captchas pose significant challenges for computer solvers. Our approach relies on fewer real captchas to construct the solver, and can target a wide range of schemes. As a result, our approach needs less human involvement compared to prior methods.

Security Features

The security features of the target scheme are used to learn a captcha generation model. This model is then applied to automatically generate synthetic captchas (with and without confusing background patterns) to learn a pre-processing model to remove security features.

Training

Our approach involves training a captcha solver using a small set of real captchas and the security features of the target scheme. The solver is then used to break captchas in various categories, including text, image, and hybrid captchas.

Robustness
